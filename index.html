UNTIL YOU RECEIVE CARE

General temporary steps

  1. 1

    Rinse gently with warm water.

  2. 2

    Avoid chewing on the affected side.

  3. 3

    Use a cold compress on the outside of the face for swelling.

  4. 4

    Do not place aspirin directly on the tooth or gums.

These are temporary educational measures, not treatment. Do not delay urgent professional care.
